#### total sum of squares
> [!info] Note: #card
> ✅ model ⇾ TSS ≈ ESS >> RSS
$$
\mathrm{TSS}=\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_{i}-{\bar{y}})^{2}
$$
$$
TSS = ESS + RSS
$$
Total variance = Explained + unexplained(noise) variance
$$
\sum_{i=1}^{N}(y[i]-\overline{{{y}}})^{2}=\sum_{i=1}^{N}(\hat{y}[i]-\overline{{{y}}})^{2}+\sum_{i=1}^{N}(y[i]-\widehat{y}[i])^{2}
$$
